My Friends

My friends and I can constantly be found playing pick up basketball, watching football or playing video games. I met the majority of my friends through youth sports and we remanded very close through high school and into college. During High School, my friends and I organized a pick up game of football to raise money for a local police officer with cancer. The game was high school students versus the Wrentham MA Police Department in a game of flag football. The entire community came out for this game and we also got Patriots DE Rob Ninkovich to do the coin flip. This was a great night for a great cause and a very proud moment for my friends and I.
